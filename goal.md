# Accountabuddy — Product Roadmap

## Phase 1: Prove the Core Loop (Now → 10 DAU)

**Gate: Do not proceed until all boxes are checked.**

- [ ] 10 users have signed up organically (not just friends you asked)
- [ ] At least 5 users completed check-ins on 3+ different days
- [ ] At least 3 users have active accountability partners (not just solo)
- [ ] You've received unsolicited positive feedback from at least 2 users
- [ ] 7-day retention rate is above 30% (3 of 10 come back after a week)
- [ ] Core flow is bug-free: signup → add partner → daily check-in → streak

**Focus:** Fix friction, talk to users, iterate on the daily check-in experience.

---

## Phase 2: Make It Shareable (10 → 50 Users)

**Gate: Do not proceed until all boxes are checked.**

- [ ] Invite/referral flow exists (share link, invite partner by email/link)
- [ ] Landing page clearly explains what Accountabuddy is in 5 seconds
- [ ] Onboarding takes under 2 minutes from signup to first check-in
- [ ] At least 20% of new users came from existing user referrals
- [ ] 50 total registered users
- [ ] 15+ weekly active users
- [ ] You've done 5+ user interviews and documented common feedback

**Focus:** Growth loops, onboarding polish, reduce drop-off points.

---

## Phase 3: Retention & Engagement (50 → 200 Users)

**Gate: Do not proceed until all boxes are checked.**

- [ ] Streak system is live and users mention it as motivation
- [ ] Push notifications or email reminders for missed check-ins
- [ ] Users can customize their goal categories
- [ ] 30-day retention rate is above 20%
- [ ] At least 10 users have streaks of 14+ days
- [ ] Weekly active users consistently above 50
- [ ] You have basic analytics tracking (sign-ups, check-ins, retention)

**Focus:** Keep people coming back. Notifications, streaks, personalization.

---

## Phase 4: Social & Gamification (200 → 1,000 Users)

**Gate: Do not proceed until all boxes are checked.**

- [ ] Cheer/encourage system is actively used (not just built)
- [ ] Leaderboards or group challenges exist
- [ ] Users can form accountability groups (not just pairs)
- [ ] Community features: shared goals, public check-ins (opt-in)
- [ ] 200+ registered users, 80+ weekly active
- [ ] At least 3 organic mentions on social media or forums
- [ ] Revenue model identified (even if not implemented)

**Focus:** Social proof, community, make accountability feel fun not forced.

---

## Phase 5: Merit-Based Validation (1,000+ Users)

**Gate: Do not proceed until all boxes are checked.**

- [ ] 1,000+ registered users with 300+ weekly active
- [ ] Existing trust signals: users already validate each other informally
- [ ] You've prototyped the merit/validation concept with 20 power users
- [ ] Legal/privacy review of decentralized validation implications
- [ ] Technical architecture documented and reviewed
- [ ] Funding or sustainable revenue to support development
- [ ] Users are explicitly asking for proof-of-work validation

**Focus:** Decentralized validation, merit scores, portable accountability reputation.

---

## Principles

1. **Don't build Phase N+1 features until Phase N gates are met.**
2. **Talk to users more than you write code.**
3. **Retention beats acquisition — a small engaged group > a large ghost town.**
4. **Every feature should make the daily check-in loop better or stickier.**
5. **The blockchain-style merit system is the endgame, not the starting point.**
